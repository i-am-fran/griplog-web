Griplog is a private logbook for tracking your climbing projects, planning trips, and documenting beta. Log routes from any discipline, indoor or outdoor.
Your entire climbing life in one app.

Create your collection

You curate your own routes. Save the projects that inspire you, the problems that challenge you, and the routes you want to remember. Track what matters to you, not everything you touch.

Plan your trips

Create events for climbing trips or gym competitions. Link routes to events. Track what you want to project and what you've sent. Organise your climbing beyond simple logging.

Built for Speed

We follow Apple's design guidelines to make the app feel native and fast. The interface gets out of your way. No gamification. No streaks, achievements, or artificial goals.
