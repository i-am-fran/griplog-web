Our Manifesto

All of our apps are turning into social networks. Your climbing became content. Your projects became posts. Progress turned into performance.

Griplog is different. It's a private logbook that does one thing well: help you track your climbing.

For You, Not for Feeds

Log any route from any discipline. Bouldering, sport climbing, trad, deep water solo. Indoor or outdoor.

Your entire climbing life in one app, kept private unless you choose to share specific routes with friends.

Built for Speed

We follow Apple's design guidelines to make sure the app feels native and fast. The interface gets out of your way.

No gamification. No streaks, achievements, or artificial goals designed to keep you opening the app.

You're in Control

Track everything or only what matters to you. Use it to plan trips, study beta, or remember where you've been. It's your tool, use it your way.

Your data belongs to you. Export it whenever you want. Delete it if you need to. Full ownership, always.
